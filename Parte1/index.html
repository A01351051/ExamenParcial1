Tribute to Nikola Tesla

TIMELINE

10,1859 Nikola Tesla is born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire, Tesla was the fourth child in a family of five children. The family comprised of three sisters including Marica, Angelina, Milka and his old brother who was known as Dane.

1881 He moved to Budapest in Hungary, to work for a telegraph company. Upon arriving, he noticed that the Budapest Telephone Exchange company was not in operation, so he worked at draftsman in another company. A few months later, the BTS Company was in operation, and he was given the position of chief electrical engineer.

1887 Tesla came up with an induction motor that was operated by alternating current, a form of power that was becoming common in Europe and the USA. This type of power provided various benefits, especially in long-distance power transmission.

1891 At the age of 35, he became a naturalized citizen of the US. In the same year, he also patented his famous Tesla Coil.
